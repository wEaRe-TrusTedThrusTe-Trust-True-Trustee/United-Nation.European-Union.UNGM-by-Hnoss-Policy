## 9. NON-COMPLIANCE PROCEDURES

### 9.1 Violation Categories

| Severity | Example | Response Time | Escalation |
|----------|---------|---------------|------------|
| Critical | Data breach, sanctions violation | <4 hours | CEO + Board |
| High | Treaty non-compliance, AML failure | <24 hours | Legal Council |
| Medium | Policy deviation, missed audit | <72 hours | Compliance Officer |
| Low | Documentation error, minor delay | <7 days | Department Head |

### 9.2 Remediation Protocol

1. **Detection**: Automated alert or manual discovery
2. **Assessment**: Compliance officer evaluates severity
3. **Containment**: Immediate action to limit impact
4. **Investigation**: Root cause analysis
5. **Remediation**: Corrective measures implemented
6. **Documentation**: Full incident report filed
7. **Prevention**: Process improvements deployed

### 9.3 Disciplinary Action

For willful non-compliance by personnel:

- **First Offense**: Written warning + mandatory training
- **Second Offense**: Performance improvement plan
- **Third Offense**: Termination + legal review (if criminal)

**Whistleblower Protection**: No retaliation against good-faith reporters.
